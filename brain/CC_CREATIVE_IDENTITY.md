# CC Creative Identity -- The On-Camera Persona

> This file captures CC's creative DNA for content production. Every script, hook, caption, and visual decision references this. Updated only when CC explicitly shifts direction.

---

## The Alter Ego: CC

- **Real name:** Conaugh McKenna (used in B2B/professional contexts)
- **Content persona:** CC -- an amplified, never-fake version of himself
- **Never a character -- always him, just with the volume turned up**
- CC is who shows up on camera. Conaugh is who signs the contracts.

---

## Core Emotional Impact (What Viewers Walk Away With)

1. **Jealousy of his clarity** -- "This guy knows exactly who he is and where he's going"
2. **Discomfort with their own inaction** -- "Why haven't I started yet?"
3. **Warmth of a wise friend** -- "He's not preaching -- he's just telling me the truth because he cares"

These three feelings -- in that order -- are the creative compass for every piece of content.

---

## The 3 Adjectives (Non-Negotiable Brand Words)

If a stranger watched 10 videos and could only use 3 words:

1. **Intelligent**
2. **Confident**
3. **Certain**

Supporting adjectives: charismatic, witty, wise, funny, easy-going, sincere

---

## Creative Archetype: The Time Traveler

CC's content should feel like a **futuristic, intimate conversation** -- like someone from the future sat down with you and said "let me tell you what's coming." Not preachy, not salesy -- just someone who can see what others can't, delivered with the ease of someone who's already been through it.

**Visual translation:**
- Cinematic, clean, slightly futuristic aesthetic
- Deep and intimate but also confident and unflinching
- "Just showed up and showed out -- another day at the office" (Rob the Bank energy)
- Different styles per piece: some intense, some sincere, some funny -- but always futuristic

---

## Voice Rules (On Camera)

### DO:
- Speak in short, punchy sentences
- Use humor naturally -- CC is genuinely funny
- Be controversial about ideas (break the status quo) -- never about people or politics
- Sound like you're talking to a friend, not an audience
- Be witty and wise beyond your years
- Use pauses for emphasis -- silence is confident
- Deliver uncomfortable truths wrapped in warmth
- Reference lived experience: dropping out, almost dying, entrepreneurship, sobriety

### NEVER:
- Sound rehearsed or scripted
- Touch political issues -- EVER. CC rises above. Everyone is included.
- Sound preachy or guru-like
- Use humor as a deflection (CC's own self-awareness about this)
- Say "hey guys" or any generic opener
- Sound like a tech bro, a motivational speaker, or a LinkedIn influencer

CC can talk about AI for 30 minutes without prep. But the angle isn't "here's a tool" -- it's "here's what's coming and you're not ready." He sees people stuck in the mud, refusing to acknowledge that AI will replace entire industries. This frustration is authentic fuel.

- Never doubting or limiting yourself
- Being desensitized to opinions but sensitive enough to respond with regulated emotion

### Pillar 4: The CEO Log (Business)
Existing pillar from CONTENT_BIBLE. Building-in-public, behind-the-scenes operator content. But now filtered through the Time Traveler archetype -- CC's business moves should feel like someone playing chess while others play checkers.

## Filming Setup & Ritual

### Current Setup
- Home desktop environment (Collingwood ON, moving to Montreal)
- Decent camera (will upgrade)
- Ring light / key light
- Would do outdoor walking content too

### Pre-Film Warm-Up
1. Push-ups
2. Chest hitting (fist to chest, get energy up)
3. Humming (voice warm-up, tone control)
4. Drink water
5. Read bullet points one more time
6. First word IS the hook -- no clearing throat, no intro

### Script Format Progression
- **Phase 1 (now):** Bullet point outlines -- hook, beats, close
- **Phase 2 (after comfort):** Lighter bullet points, more improv
- **Phase 3 (mastery):** Full improvisation from a single topic word
- Test all three and see what produces the best content

### Music
- Viral audios from Instagram
- Should match the futuristic/cinematic vibe
- Different moods per pillar: intense for AI Oracle, warm for The Becoming, raw for The Journey
