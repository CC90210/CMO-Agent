**Hook (Look directly into the camera. No intro.):**
"Everyone tells you not to quit when it gets hard. Nobody warns you about the danger of quitting when it gets easy."

**Beat 1 (The Confession):**
"If I’m being completely honest with you... I have a massive problem with complacency. As soon as I do something hard and I overcome it, the challenge disappears. It becomes easy. And the second it becomes easy, I take my foot off the gas and I lose my momentum."

**Beat 2 (The Reality Check):**
"Recently, I started smoking weed again. And it completely hijacked my proactive thinking. I caught myself sitting around watching entertainment nonsense, getting paranoid, and losing the edge that built my business in the first place. I was sedating myself because I had hit a pocket of comfort."

**Beat 3 (The Core Mentality):**
"So I'm cutting it out to lock back in. Because I realized something fundamental: Do not quit when it gets painful, and do not quit when it gets comfortable, because you must continue to grow as a person."

**Beat 4 (The Desired State):**
"My standard isn't just making it through the day. My standard is a perpetual state of enthusiasm, charisma, and certainty. And you cannot maintain that state if you let yourself go numb the second you get a win."

**Close (Hard cut on the final word):**
"We are staying on the gas. Only good things from now on."
