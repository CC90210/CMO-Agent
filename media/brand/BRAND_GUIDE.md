### Primary Font: Inter
- **Headings:** Inter Bold (700), tracking -0.02em
- **Subheadings:** Inter SemiBold (600)
- **Body:** Inter Regular (400), line-height 1.6

## 4. Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality
- **Confident but not arrogant** — We know our value. We don't need to oversell.
- **Direct but personable** — Business conversations that feel like talking to a smart friend.
- **Technical but accessible** — We explain AI in terms of hours saved and money earned.
- **Ambitious but authentic** — We share the real journey, not a polished fantasy.

### Do's
- ✅ Lead with specific, tangible outcomes ("Save 15 hours/week")
- ✅ Use real examples from our actual stack
- ✅ Sound like a human, not a corporation
- ✅ Make the reader feel something (curiosity, urgency, possibility)
- ✅ Back up claims with specifics

### Don'ts
- ❌ No hustle-culture language ("grind," "rise and grind," "10x")
- ❌ No vague AI hype ("leverage cutting-edge synergies")
- ❌ No motivational poster energy
- ❌ No apologetic tone ("we're just a small company...")
- ❌ No overselling — underpromise, overdeliver

### Sample Voice Lines
- "I replaced 3 part-time admin roles with a system that runs 24/7."
- "Your competitors are automating right now. We make sure you do it better."
- "10 hours of admin work, gone. What would you do with that time?"
