# Agency — Lead Channels That Work Sub-$50K MRR

> For consultancies, marketing agencies, dev shops, fractional execs, and boutique studios. Applies to OASIS AI (CC's agency) directly.

**Canonical voices**: Blair Enns (*Win Without Pitching*), David C. Baker (*The Business of Expertise*), Aaron Ross (*Predictable Revenue*), Hormozi (*$100M Leads*), Chris Do (The Futur).

---

## The 4 channels ranked for sub-$50K MRR agencies

Agencies are a high-AOV, low-volume, trust-driven business. Ranking is deliberately different from e-commerce.

### 1. Warm outreach + referrals (HIGHEST ROI at this stage)
- **Why it works**: agency buyers trust people, not ads. Referrals close at 3-5x inbound rates (Baker's data).
- **How**: systematic ask at 30-day client success milestone. Give-give-ask structure (Adam Grant). Dream 100 targeting for referral sources, not just prospects.
- **OASIS-specific**: CC's existing network → PULSE case studies → warm intro ask. Skool community (158 members) is the gold vein.
- **Target**: 50% of new clients from referrals at sub-$50K MRR.

### 2. Authority content (Enns + Baker canon)
- **Why it works**: Enns — agencies become "the expert you already trusted" via expertise display, not pitch display. Baker — positioning so specific that referrals include the specialty.
- **How**:
  - LinkedIn long-form (Justin Welsh model, but agency-flavor)
  - Podcast guesting on 10-20 podcasts where ICP listens (not your own — appear on others')
  - Weekly newsletter to the list of 500 right people, not 50,000 wrong ones
- **OASIS-specific**: CC's personal brand → OASIS thought-leadership. Every piece of PULSE content is authority-building for OASIS.
- **Lead metric**: qualified inbound DMs + booked discovery calls. Not followers.

### 3. Partnerships / sub-contracting
- **Why it works**: larger agencies outsource work they don't want. You get pre-qualified, pre-priced work with no sales cycle.
- **How**: identify 10-20 agencies one tier larger than you, whose specialty complements yours. Reach out with "we sub-contract and white-label if useful."
- **Risk**: margin compression (you'll be at 50-60% of their retail). Good bridge while building direct pipeline.

### 4. Cold outbound (Predictable Revenue, Ross)
- **Why it works**: systematic, measurable, scalable. Works best above $10K MRR when you have case studies.
- **How**: separate SDR role from closer role (Ross's key insight). Quality over volume — 20 hyper-personalized emails > 200 templated.
- **Tooling**: Clay.com for enrichment, Apollo for data, Instantly for sending. Warm up domains. Cap at 30-50/day per sender.
- **Anti-pattern**: spray-and-pray. Agencies who look spammy burn their domain + brand. Only cold-outbound when you have (a) proof, (b) a specific offer, (c) a reason THIS prospect specifically.

### 5. Paid ads (DE-PRIORITIZED for agencies sub-$50K MRR)
- **Why it's last**: agency buyers don't click ads. They search after a referral, or they respond to a cold email from someone whose content they've read.
- **When to turn it on**: above $50K MRR, when you have (a) clear positioning, (b) case studies, (c) a tight-intent funnel (lead magnet → case study → call). Not before.
- **What works when you do**: LinkedIn Sponsored InMail (expensive, works for >$25K ACV), Meta retargeting of website visitors (cheap, closes the loop), branded search Google Ads (defensive).

## The Dream 100 (Holmes + Brunson, adapted for agencies)

List 100 people who already have your ideal clients' attention:
- **Agencies**: podcasters CC's ICP listens to, newsletter operators they subscribe to, YouTubers they follow, community operators (Skool / Circle / Discord) they participate in.
- **Weekly cadence**: engage with 5/week thoughtfully. NEVER pitch in public replies. Build familiarity over 6 months. Direct outreach after 6-10 meaningful public touches.

---

## Agency-specific capture tooling

- **Discovery call = the capture event.** Calendly / SavvyCal / Cal.com direct booking. Bypass form-then-book friction.
- **Pre-call teaser email** (Enns's "insight asset") lands in inbox within 5 minutes of booking. Demonstrates competence before the call.
- **No "contact us" forms.** Always offer direct booking OR direct email response. Friction kills agency leads.

## What to measure

| Metric | OASIS sub-$50K MRR target |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Discovery calls booked / month | 12-20 |
| Show rate | >75% (pre-call teaser is the conversion lever) |
| Discovery → proposal | >60% |
| Proposal → win | >35% |
| Referral rate (new clients from existing) | >40% |
| Avg. touches before first call | 7-12 (authority content compounds) |

---

## Channel kill criteria

- Cold outbound sending for 6 weeks with <5% positive reply rate → positioning or offer is off, don't fix with more volume
- Paid ads >$150 CPQL with <25% SQL conversion for 3 weeks → pause, diagnose positioning first
- Podcast guesting 10 episodes with 0 leads → wrong shows, re-research audience first
